# Chicken Bacon Ranch Pizza

* 1 lb chicken breasts
* 4-5 strips of bacon
* 4 naan flatbread
* 1/2 cup jalapeno ranch or regular ranch
* 16 oz shredded mozzarella cheese

1. Cook chicken and chop
1. Cook bacon and crumble
1. Place naan bread on a hot griddle and warm both sides. Flip so the flat side is down (textured side facing up).
1. Spread a generous layer of ranch dressing on the textured side of each naan. Top with shredded mozzarella, chopped chicken, and bacon.
1. Add a splash of water to the griddle and cover the flatbreads with a dome or lid. Steam until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
1. Remove from heat, slice into pieces, and enjoy!
