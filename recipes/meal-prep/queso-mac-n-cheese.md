# Queso Mac n' Cheese

* 2 red bell peppers
* 2 yellow bell peppers
* 1 onion
* 32 oz chicken breast, cubed
* 120 G red enchilada sauce
* 80 G green chiles
* 2 tbsp garlic puree
* 14 oz can of fire roasted tomatoes
* 2 tsp onion powder
* 1 tbsp salt
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
* 672 G pasta

Blended queso sauce:
* 800 G 2% cottage cheese
* 100 G 1/3 fat cream cheese
* 150 G extra sharp cheddar cheese
* 30 G cheddar powder
* 120 G red enchilada sauce
* 150 G milk

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
1. Dice bell peppers and onion and bake for 20-25 minutes
1. Add chicken, enchilada sauce, green chiles, garlic puree, roasted tomatoes, and the spices to the crock pot
1. Add cooked peppers and onions to the crock pot
1. Stir
1. Cook on low for 3-4 hours or high for 2-3 hours

    **NOTE:** Do these next steps about 30 minutes before the end time on the crock pot
1. Parcook the pasta (about half way done)
1. Put together the blended queso sauce (no instructions, so just need to figure it out. It should look like soup. Saw someone suggested heating it all up on the stove)
1. Add pasta and blended queso sauce to crock pot and stir
1. Cover and cook on high for 15-20 minutes

Should make about 10 containers of food. Can freeze and will keep for month. Microwave for about 4 minutes. Can add a little milk after reheating for a creamier sauce.
