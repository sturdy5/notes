# Grape Jelly

* 3 cups grape juice
* 1 package pectin powder
* 4 cups sugar
* 5 half pint mason jars

1. Put mason jars into a big pot and cover with water
1. Put the pot on the stove on low, just to keep the temperature up
1. Wisk pectin and the grape juice in a sauce pan and bring to a boil
1. Reduce heat and wisk in sugar until fully combined
1. Bring back to a boil. Be careful here, if it comes to a boil too quickly it will boil over and make a giant mess.
1. Boil for 1 minute and remove from heat
1. Skim foam off the top
1. Laddle into jars, leaving 3/4" of head room
1. Wipe top of jars with water and put lids on
1. Flip jars upside down on the counter and let cool completely

This will make jelly that is stable in the fridge for a couple months.
