## Chocolate Chip Cookies

* 1 cup (2 sticks) softened butter (preferably salted)
* 1 cup granulated sugar
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 2 tsp vanilla extract
* 2 large eggs
* 3 cups all-purpose flour
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 2 cups chocolate chips (semi-sweet)

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
1. In a medium bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside
1. Cream together the butter and the sugars in a mixer
1. Beat in the eggs and the vanilla (about 1 minute)
1. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined
1. Add in the chocolate chips and mix well
1. Using a scooper, roll out about 30 balls of cookie dough and evenly place them on cookie sheets (typically 3)
1. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes. Take them out when they are just **barely** starting to turn brown
1. Let them sit on the baking sheet for about 5 minutes prior to moving to a cooling rack
