## Sandwich Bread

In the stand mixer bowl

* 2 cups of warm water
* 1 tbsp active dry yeast
* 1 tbsp honey

Stir and let the yeast do its thing. Once it is nice and foamy

* 2 tbsp melted butter
* 2 tbsp of salt
* 1/4 cup honey

stir

* 5 cups of flour

Use the mixed with the dough hook to mix it up. It is ready when the dough no longer sticks to the side of the bowl and becomes one big ball.

Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise for 60-90 minutes - until dough is roughly double the size

Separate the dough into two sections. For each section, flatten to a rectange (not too thin) and fold in half. Turn 90 degress fold up like towels - into thirds.

Grease two loaf pans liberally

Put one loaf into each loaf pan. Cover and let rise again - roughly 30 minutes.

Bake at 350 for 35 minutes

Once it comes out of the oven, spread butter on the top
